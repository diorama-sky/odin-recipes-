Ingredients

  • 1 package lavash flatbread
  • 1/2 cup pizza sauce
  • 8 ounces mozzarella cheese, shredded
  • 2 ounces pepperoni, or as needed

Directions

  1. Preheat the oven to 350 degrees F (180 degrees C). Spray one side of lavash with nonstick spray.
  2. Bake lavash directly on the rack in the preheated oven for 2 minutes
    3. .
  3. Carefully remove lavash from oven; place on a baking tray.
  4. Spread 2 to 3 tablespoons pizza sauce on each lavash.
  5. Sprinkle 2 ounces shredded mozzarella cheese on each lavash. Top with pepperoni.
  6. Bake pizza until cheese is melted and golden, about 15 minutes.
